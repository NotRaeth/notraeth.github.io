General Rules

Scorestreaks

  • 20 Minute grace period prior to the start of the match. If all the players are not in the lobby by this time, the first map is given to the only full team. If both teams are missing players, the team with less players in the lobby loses the map.
  • After the first map is awarded, ten minutes will be allowed for players to attempt to join, if the players do not join in time, the map will be awarded the same as before. This will continue until a team wins the series.
  • Third-Person Spectating: DISABLED
  • Revenge Voice: DISABLED
  • Battlechatter: DISABLED
  • Listen-Ins Will not Occur, chat programs may be used by the teams discretion.

Hardpoint

Hardpoint will be played the same as it has been in previous years. There will be no re-spawn delay.
In addition, there will be a selection of seven maps, as follows:

  1. Arsenal
  2. Frequency
  3. Hacienda
  4. Slums
  5. Payload
  6. Militia
  7. Seaside

Search & Destroy

Search & Destroy will be played the same as it has been in previous years. It will be a first to six match.
In addition, there will be a selection of seven maps, as follows:

  1. Gridlock
  2. Seaside
  3. Militia
  4. Arsenal
  5. Frequency
  6. Hacienda
  7. Payload

Control

Control will be played as a first to 5 instead of a first to 3 in order to allow for a better back and forth interaction.

  1. Gridlock
  2. Seaside
